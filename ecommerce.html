E-Commerce Website Engineering

In the 21st century it is imperative that any business engaged in sales do so over the Internet. In the 1970's Microsoft launched its "A Computer In Every Home" marketing campaign. It was nonetheless successful in the western world. Today Android and Apple are putting smart-phones and tablets in the hands of every consumer; while Verizon and its competitors see to their global connectivity. The fact is a Brick and Mortar store can only see so much foot traffic in a day. But an online store can handle near infinite visitors.

For Your Guests

Your guests will enjoy an online catalog comprising of quick and secure payment processing and order tracking. Their account management will let them keep track of past purchases and sort their current carts. If they encounter an issue the application gives them the ability to reach out to your customer service for help via telephone, e-mail, or a Frequently Asked Questions document. All of this is available in sleek and disability-accessible easy to use designs.

For Your Business

You and your team will have a separate portal to track orders to update your deliverable records and make sure that your courier is on track for your customer satisfaction. It will also include a quarterly growth and sales tracking system that will let you manage your inventory and entice your guests to visit based on their purchase history.
